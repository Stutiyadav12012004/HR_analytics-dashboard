# HR Analytics Dashboard - Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes employee attrition and helps identify the key factors contributing to employee turnover. The dashboard provides insights into workforce demographics, job roles, salary levels, education backgrounds, and years of service to support data-driven HR decision-making.

## Business Problem

Employee attrition can negatively impact organizational performance, increase recruitment costs, and reduce productivity. This project aims to identify patterns and trends behind employee resignations and help HR teams develop effective retention strategies.

## Tools & Technologies Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Employees
- Total Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate (%)
- Average Age
- Average Salary
- Average Years at Company

## Dashboard Features

### Attrition by Education
Analyzes employee attrition across different educational backgrounds.

### Attrition by Gender
Compares attrition rates between male and female employees.

### Attrition by Age Group
Identifies age groups with the highest employee turnover.

### Attrition by Job Role
Highlights job positions experiencing the highest attrition.

### Attrition by Salary Slab
Shows the relationship between salary levels and employee turnover.

### Attrition by Years at Company
Examines how employee tenure affects attrition behavior.

## Key Insights

- Employees with lower salary levels show higher attrition rates.
- Certain job roles experience significantly higher turnover than others.
- Most attrition occurs during the early years of employment.
- Attrition patterns vary across education levels and gender groups.
- Younger employees demonstrate relatively higher turnover rates.

## Business Recommendations

- Improve employee engagement programs for new hires.
- Review compensation structures for lower salary bands.
- Develop retention strategies for high-turnover job roles.
- Implement career development and training opportunities.
- Conduct regular employee satisfaction surveys.
